Guide: How to Use the Lesson Recommender Demo

This demo showcases a recommendation engine for language learners, built on precalculated embeddings and cosine similarities, with weighted attributes (60% semantic title similarity, 30% language, 10% CEFR). Recommendations are generated based on the semantic similarities between lesson titles, using your three most recent bookings. Real-world engines are far more complex, incorporating advanced methods and models – for a tailored solution, hire me ✉ as a professional!

• Explore Lessons: View all lessons in the table, showing each lesson’s title, language, and CEFR level (A1 to C2).

• Book a Lesson: Click the "Book" button to add a lesson to your bookings (up to three). You’ll see a checkmark and "Booked" once booked.

• View Booked Lessons: See your booked lessons below, listed with the booking time (latest on top). To start over, reload the page.

• Get Recommendations: After booking, view AI-driven recommendations based on your recent bookings.
